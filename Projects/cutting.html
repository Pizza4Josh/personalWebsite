Various Lazer Cutting

Personal Project

Overview

From: November 2025 To: Feburary 2026

Over the 2025-2026 summer, I was working at University doing the weeding robot project with PRL. I had access to the lazer cutting lab and had free time in the evenings...

The first thing I made was picture engraved cutting boards as a christmas present for my partner. I then cut ~15 keychains out of left over red acrylic with my friends and family's names and numbers.

Tools and skills

  • CAD design in SolidWorks, exporting as DXF to lazer cutter.
  • Correct tuning of speed and power to engrave photos and text.

Limitations and Improvements

  • The chopping boards varied greatly in quality, and in extent, the amount that they burned. As you can see, the bush image came out far worse than the face one.
  • I ended up putting the wrong phone number on two key chains which was embarrasing!
